About Amazing Grace High School

Established in 1998, Amazing Grace High School stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Nigeria. We are committed to nurturing young minds through a comprehensive approach that combines academic rigor with character development.

Our institution has consistently produced outstanding graduates who excel in universities and contribute meaningfully to society. With state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated faculty, we provide an environment where students can discover their potential and pursue their dreams.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and dynamic educational environment where students excel academically and morally, developing critical thinking skills and strong character foundation for lifelong success.

Our Vision

To raise globally competent and compassionate leaders through quality education founded on Christian values, preparing students to make positive contributions to their communities and the world.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Compassion, Innovation, and Respect guide everything we do, creating a supportive community where every student can thrive and reach their full potential.
